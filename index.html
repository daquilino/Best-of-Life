Welcome!

Welcome to the Best of Life app. When there are some many options to choose from for what you want it can be difficult to just choose just one. Best of Life has solved that common problem! Just type in what you are looking for and a location to search within and let statistics make the decision for you. Best of Life searches your topic and uses the best rated option, according to Yelp, and displays it one the map.

How does it work?

Best of Life uses the power of Yelp and Google Maps to make your life easier. It searches for your topic in Yelp's database, sorts the results in the location range by rating, and displays the best rated option on a map provided by Google. Also, just in case you do not like or cannot go to the best option, Best of Life provides the second best and third best options on the map for your convenience.

Don't know what to search for?

Best of Life functions in 4 main categories including restaurants, nightlife, home services, and delivery services (restaurants that deliver).

Examples of restaurants are pizza, coffee, Chinese food, sushi, Italian food, etc.

Examples of nightlife are sports bars, karaoke bars, dance clubs, etc.

Examples of home services are plumbers, electricians, general contractors, home cleaners, etc.
